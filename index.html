Our Services

Comprehensive electrical solutions for homes, businesses, and industries

Residential Electrical Installations

Complete electrical system installation for new homes or renovations. Includes wiring, outlets, switches, electrical panels, and grid connection.

Electrical Repairs

Diagnosis and solution of problems like short circuits, blackouts, non-working outlets, tripping differentials, or wiring failures.

Preventive Maintenance

Periodic inspection of installations to prevent failures or electrical fires. Includes checking connections, loads, and wire condition.

Electrical Panel Installation

Installation or renovation of electrical panels with circuit breakers, differentials, and overload protection.

Interior Lighting

Design and installation of lamps, LED lights, decorative lighting, recessed lighting, and low-consumption systems.

Exterior Lighting

Installation of lights for patios, gardens, facades, terraces, or parking lots, including motion sensor systems.

Home Automation

Installation of smart systems to control lights, outlets, climate, or blinds from mobile devices or voice assistants.

EV Charger Installation

Installation of charging points in homes, garages, or businesses for electric vehicles.

Security Systems

Electrical connection for surveillance cameras, alarms, motion sensors, and access control systems.

Doorbells & Video Intercoms

Installation of electric doorbells, automatic intercoms, or video intercoms in homes and buildings.

Ceiling Fans

Mounting and safe electrical connection of ceiling fans with or without lighting.

Electrical Certificates

Inspection of installations and issuance of certificates required to contract electricity or sell/rent a property.

Additional Outlets

Expansion of electrical points in homes or businesses for greater comfort or new needs.

Solar Energy Systems

Electrical connection of solar panels, inverters, and batteries for self-consumption.

Commercial Installations

Electrical design and installation in stores, restaurants, offices, or shops complying with safety regulations.

Generator Installation

Connection of backup generators for homes or businesses in case of power outages.

LED Efficient Lighting

Replacement of old systems with LED lighting to save energy and reduce electricity costs.
